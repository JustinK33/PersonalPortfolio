About Me

Computer Science sophomore at Rutgers with a focus on backend and distributed systems. The deeper I get into these systems, the more I want to understand what's happening underneath — how data stays consistent, how things fail gracefully, and how you design for load you can't fully predict.

My internship experience was mostly building and shipping backend features end to end. That's where I started caring about the harder questions: what breaks at scale, and why. I got comfortable with the gap between "it works" and "it works reliably."

Right now I'm drawn to problems around fault tolerance, consistency, and system design — not just implementing features, but understanding the tradeoffs behind the decisions that make a system actually hold up in production.
